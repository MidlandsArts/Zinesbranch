BOA Digital Technologies

This zine created by BOA Digital Technologies highlights the students concerns about pollution and how that is damaging our planet, they tell us to ‘act now’ to make a difference. The zine includes both photographs of their initial physical zines and 3D paper sculptures made in the first workshop, alongside digital sketches and designed layouts. You can see a short film the students directed and edited, which uses clever editing techniques to create impact in their message.
