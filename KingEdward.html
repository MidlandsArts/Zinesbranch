King Edward VI Five Ways School

This zine reflects the DIY approach to zine making, featuring collage work created on paper with magazines and other found materials, alongside digital drawing and photography. The children created fun avatars using their photographs and free clip art as they explored the possibilities of the digital equipment they were using. The students worked with a professional rapper to create pledge ‘raps’ using garage band which you can listen to throughout the zine. 

On the final workshop the students created a collaborative work taking photographs using a framing device which was transformed in to an animated film. The film references the Reframe project making us think about changing our view.
